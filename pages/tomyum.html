Spaghetti Bolognese

Nutrition Facts (per serving)
    152 Calories
    7g Fat
    19g Carbs
    4g Protein
Ingredients
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 pound raw shrimp
  • 4 limes
  • 2 stalks lemongrass
  • 4 slices ginger root
  • 2 tablespoons tom yum paste
  • 1 ½ tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chile sauce
  • 6 baby bella mushrooms
  • 2 green onions

    • Tom Yum

    (13k Reviews)

      How to Cook:

    1. Combine vegetable broth and shrimp in a large pot over medium-high heat. Bring to a low boil for 10 minutes, then lower heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove shrimp from broth; remove and discard shells. Set cooked shrimp aside.
    2. Add lime juice, chile peppers, lemongrass, ginger, tom yum paste, fish sauce, sugar, and chile sauce to broth; cook for 10 minutes. Remove chile peppers, lemongrass, and ginger slices with a slotted spoon and discard. Return cooked shrimp to broth and remove the pot from heat.
    3. Ladle soup into bowls. Top with mushrooms and green onions.
